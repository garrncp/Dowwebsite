Documents to prepare

1. Passport (At least 6 months remaining) (in case of issuing a new passport) * 5 years 1,000 baht *10 years 1500 baht
2. Copy of passport
3. Physical examination sheet (price 1700 baht)
4. Certificate of student status (English version)
5. Transcripts (English version)
6. Taiwan University Application Form
7. Acceptance letter from Taiwan University

Taipei Economic and Cultural Office

1. Online petition form
2. Two photographs with white background, size 2 inches
3 Acceptance letter from Taiwan University, original + copy
4. Evidence of financial statements from the bank (Parent) for the past 6 months **Account balance must not be less than 80000 baht
5. Air ticket
6. Original ID card + copy
7. Real house
